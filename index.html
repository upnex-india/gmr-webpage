Discover Araku Valley

Nestled among the Eastern Ghats, Araku is a stunning hill station known for its aromatic coffee plantations, breathtaking waterfalls like Chaparai and Katiki, vibrant tribal culture, and serene botanical gardens. Here at Good Morning Resorts, we bring you the best of this tranquil paradise with comfortable accommodation and warm hospitality.
